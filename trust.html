Privacy policy

How we handle your data.

CarbonOS processes data on behalf of customers under a Data Processing Agreement. We never sell personal data. We retain customer data only as long as the customer relationship requires, plus the legal retention window. We support data subject access requests through our customer-controlled UI and via privacy@carbonos.io.

Sub-processors

A current list of sub-processors is published at carbonos.io/sub-processors and updated with 30 days notice.

Data residency

Customers can elect US, EU or APAC residency on Business plans, and additional regions on Enterprise. All data, backups and processing remain within the elected region.

Your rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, UK GDPR and equivalent regimes, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, portability and objection. We respond to requests within statutory windows and at no cost.

Terms of service

CarbonOS terms.

By creating a CarbonOS workspace, you agree to our Terms of Service. The summary: you can use the platform for any lawful carbon market activity; you retain ownership of your project, transaction and counterparty data; we provide the platform on an enterprise SLA; either side can end the engagement with the agreed notice. Full terms are at carbonos.io/terms.

Service Level Agreement

Business and Enterprise plans carry a 99.9% monthly uptime SLA with service credits for missed targets. Enterprise can negotiate a custom SLA up to 99.99% with regional DR.

Acceptable use

The platform may not be used to facilitate fraud, sanctioned-party transactions, or transactions that violate the integrity standards of integrated registries (Verra, Gold Standard, ACR, CAR, ICVCM CCPs).

Data Processing Agreement

DPA & SCCs.
