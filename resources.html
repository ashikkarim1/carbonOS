Glossary

The carbon markets glossary.

A working vocabulary for 400+ industry terms — plain English, with cross-references and authoritative sources.

Additionality

Whether emissions reductions or removals would have occurred without the carbon finance — the foundational integrity test.

Permanence

The durability of a carbon removal or avoidance, typically expressed in years or millennia. Buffer pools insure against reversal.

Leakage

Displaced emissions occurring outside a project boundary as a consequence of the project's activities.

Corresponding adjustment

The accounting move under Article 6.2 by which a host country deducts a transferred mitigation outcome from its own inventory.

Vintage

The calendar year in which the emission reduction or removal occurred — distinct from the year of issuance.

Buffer pool

A reserve of credits set aside to cover potential reversals from permanence risk, particularly in nature-based projects.
