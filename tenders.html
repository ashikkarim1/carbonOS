Compliance-grade tenders

CORSIA, Article 6 and government procurement.

Purpose-built workflows for the specific compliance requirements of aviation carbon offset procurement, Paris Agreement Article 6 bilateral tenders and government climate procurement programs.

ICAO CORSIA

Airlines procuring CORSIA-eligible offsets need institutional-grade tender processes. The platform enforces CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit (CEU) eligibility, programme approval status, vintage requirements and first-transfer verification across all bids.

Article 6 bilateral

Governments structuring bilateral agreements under Paris Article 6.2 need corresponding adjustment tracking, host-country authorization verification and ITMO compliance. The platform handles the full bilateral tender and negotiation lifecycle.

Government procurement

National and regional governments running climate procurement programs require transparent, auditable tender processes. The platform provides sealed-bid management, fairness controls, evaluation audit trails and public disclosure tooling.

Corporate offset RFPs

Fortune 500 companies running structured offset procurement processes. Portfolio-grade sourcing with methodology diversification, vintage management and CSRD/SBTi alignment built into the evaluation criteria.

Multilateral tenders

Development banks, climate funds and multilateral institutions running large-scale carbon credit procurement. Multi-tranche tenders with phased evaluation, country allocation and development impact scoring.

Voluntary market tenders

Brokers, traders and aggregators running procurement across the voluntary market. Multi-standard eligibility, cross-registry matching and ICVCM Core Carbon Principles compliance verification.

AI-powered evaluation

Tender AI that scores, checks and flags — so humans decide.

The AI evaluation engine processes every submission against your criteria, checks compliance automatically, detects outliers and anomalies, and presents structured scorecards — leaving the award decision to your team.

Compliance auto-check

Every bid automatically verified against tender requirements — eligibility, documentation completeness, certification status and methodology fit.

Technical scoring

AI evaluates technical proposals against weighted criteria — additionality, permanence, co-benefits, MRV quality and delivery risk.

Commercial analysis

Price benchmarking against live market data, volume-discount modelling, delivery schedule feasibility and total cost analysis.

Outlier detection

Statistical analysis flags anomalous pricing, unusual bid patterns and potential collusion indicators for human review.

Bidder reputation

Track record scoring based on historical delivery, past project performance, registry approval rates and counterparty references.

Audit trail

Immutable, tamper-evident log of every action — bid receipt timestamps, evaluation scores, committee decisions and award rationale.

Track active tenders, bid submissions, evaluation progress, awards and compliance from a single command center. Real-time analytics show bidder engagement, price distributions and timeline adherence.
