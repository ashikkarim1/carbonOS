Common questions

Frequently asked.

What is Decarb AI OS?
Decarb AI OS is the world's decarbonisation platform — an AI-native operating system for carbon project origination, emissions strategy, environmental asset management, bid and tender operations, and carbon credit lifecycle management. It's built for project developers, enterprises, consulting firms, investors and governments.
Who is Decarb AI OS for?
Project developers with active projects or projects in development, enterprises figuring out their emissions reduction strategy, consulting firms (like Deloitte, Accenture, PwC, TechM) building sustainability practices, investors looking for carbon deal flow, and corporate buyers procuring environmental assets.
What is the tender management system?
The platform includes institutional-grade bid and tender management for carbon credit procurement — supporting both managed (full-service) and unmanaged (self-service) tenders. Purpose-built for CORSIA, government climate procurement, Article 6 bilateral tenders and corporate offset RFPs.
How does the AI work?
Decarb AI OS runs multiple specialised AI engines — each focused on a specific domain. The AI doesn't issue credits — only accredited registries can. The engines draft methodology fits, MRV plans, financial models, due-diligence memos, tender evaluations and compliance reports, all reviewed by humans before action.
Is this a marketplace?
Decarb AI OS includes a marketplace, but it's much more — it's the full operating system for the carbon lifecycle. Project origination, consulting, financing, tenders, intelligence, deal execution, compliance and reporting all run on the same platform.
Can consulting firms partner with Decarb AI OS?
Yes. We offer strategic partnership, white-label and co-development models for consulting firms and system integrators. Partners can embed our AI engines and platform infrastructure into their client engagements with revenue sharing and investment options available.
How do I get started?
Sign up on the platform — it's free to start. Our AI-guided onboarding helps you map your emissions landscape and build your first strategy framework. Enterprise and partner tracks are available with dedicated onboarding support.
