Common questions

Frequently asked.

Is CarbonOS a marketplace?
CarbonOS includes a marketplace, but it is not positioned as one. The platform is the operating system for the entire carbon lifecycle — origination, financing, validation, issuance, trading, retirement and reporting — with the marketplace as one of ten layers.
Which registries does CarbonOS integrate with?
Verra, Gold Standard, ACR, CAR, Puro.earth, Isometric, Plan Vivo, ART TREES, plus national registries through partner agreements. New registry integrations ship roughly every quarter.
How does CarbonOS price?
There are three pricing models: SaaS subscriptions per workspace (developer, buyer, enterprise), transaction fees on marketplace and capital flows, and intelligence subscriptions for the Carbon Terminal. See our pricing page for details.
Is the AI generating credits or just analyzing them?
CarbonOS AI does not issue credits — only accredited registries can. The AI engines draft methodology fits, MRV plans, baselines, financial models, due-diligence memos and registry submission packages, all reviewed by accredited humans before submission.
What's the security posture?
SOC 2 Type II audited annually, ISO 27001 certified, GDPR and SOC for Climate Disclosure aligned. SSO/SCIM, role-based access, immutable audit logs, customer-managed keys and regional data residency available for enterprise.
Can governments deploy a sovereign registry on CarbonOS?
Yes. The CarbonOS National Registry deployment package goes live in roughly 90 days, with Article 6.2 ITMO tracking, corresponding-adjustment support, jurisdictional REDD+ accounting and public transparency dashboards.
How long does onboarding take?
Self-serve workspaces for developers and consultants can be provisioned in under 30 minutes. Enterprise deployments typically run 4–8 weeks including SSO, data integrations and reporting connector setup.
