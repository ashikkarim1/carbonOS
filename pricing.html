Pricing FAQ

Common questions

Can I combine plans?
Yes. Most customers combine a workspace subscription with an Intelligence subscription. Transaction fees apply on top only when flows occur.
Is there a free trial?
Professional and Business plans include a 14-day free trial. Starter is free forever for individual users.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits, governments or research institutions?
Yes. Significant discounts are available — contact sales with proof of organization status.
What about per-tonne fees on retirements?
No additional per-tonne fee on retirements. The marketplace transaction fee at purchase time covers the full retirement lifecycle.
Can governments deploy a private instance?
Yes. Enterprise plans support fully private and white-labelled deployments, including on-premise for sovereign use.
