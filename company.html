Our mission

The carbon economy needs an operating system. We're building it.

The world will need to channel trillions of dollars into carbon projects over the next two decades — and the systems that move that capital, validate that science and deliver those credits do not yet exist. CarbonOS is the company building them.

We started from a simple observation: every other capital market — equities, fixed income, FX, commodities — has its Salesforce, its Bloomberg, its Stripe. Carbon has none of those. So we are building all of them, carbon-native and AI-first, on a single platform that is itself the network effect.
